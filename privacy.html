Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 20, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide to us directly, such as when you fill out a contact form. This information may include your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you choose to provide. We may also collect anonymous information about your browsing behavior on our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate and maintain our Service, to respond to your questions and comments, and to provide you with information about our products and services. We will not share your personal information with any third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

3. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable efforts to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You may also have other rights under applicable data protection laws. To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@yarna.ab.
